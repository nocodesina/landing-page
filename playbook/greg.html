What you will get

Direction

A clear learning path with a specific order and direction, to help you spend less time searching for answers and focus on learning what matters.

Connection with Greg

Throughout the course of the Playbook, you can share your questions in a personal channel. We will periodically share your questions with Greg and share her response with you. Please note that this is primarily a self-directed learning experience and Greg's participation is optional.

Self-directed learning

Each week, you receive a number of learning materials and tasks. It is up to you how long to commit, or when in the week to attend to them, as long as you spend at least 2 hours a week.

Peer support

The biggest problem of learning online is arguably the absence of an accountability group. We solve that problem for you via facilitating specialised, personal cohorts of learners who have similar learning goals.

Peer review

Every week you will spend 30 minutes reviewing your cohort's learning, while they review yours. Each week, you receive personal feedback (and possibly words of encouragement) from the other investors in your cohort.

Attention optimisation

Learning is hard, and your attention is scarce. We have deliberately designed this learning experience to help you stay attentive and reach your learning goals.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does this work?

When you sign up, you will be added to an online community of other Sales Development Representatives and Inside Sales People. Each week, you receive a specific learning resource, a mini task and feedback form to review your peers' progress. When you complete a week's learning experience, you unlock the next week's . Throughout the experience, you can interact with other peers in the cohorts, and with Greg.

I'm busy, does this require a lot of commitment?

Playbook's learning experience is self-paced and asynchronous, meaning you can choose your own timing, as long as you can commit the minimum number of hours each week.

Will I directly interact with Greg?

Occasionally, yes, although the experience is primarily self-directed and peer-reviewed. You receive the learning instructions and milestones from Greg, and we give you an accountability group and a structure and help you optimize your attention and time for you so you can focus on learning.
