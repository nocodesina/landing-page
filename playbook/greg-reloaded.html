Throughout the course of the Playbook, you can share your questions in a personal channel. We will periodically share your questions with Daren and share her response with you.

Self-directed learning

Each week, you receive a number of learning materials and tasks. It is up to you how long to commit, or when in the week to attend to them, as long as you spend at least 2 hours a week.

Peer support

The biggest problem of learning online is arguably the absence of an accountability group. We solve that problem for you via facilitating specialised, personal cohorts of learners who have similar learning goals.

Peer review

Every week you will spend 30 minutes reviewing your cohort's learning, while they review yours. Each week, you receive personal feedback (and possibly words of encouragement) from the other investors in your cohort.

Attention optimisation

Learning is hard, and your attention is scarce. We have deliberately designed this learning experience to help you stay attentive and reach your learning goals.
