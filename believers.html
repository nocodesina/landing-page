the culture

You're investing in a learning organization

Sina has spent the past 5 years trying to find an education company that he would personally want to work at, to solve hard unsolved problems in education, and he hasn't been able to find one.

He understands the industry's formulas for student enrolment, conversion, and recurring revenue, but can't find companies who have cracked the formula for learning, aka Pedagogy. He believes that the industry is lacking: 1. cross-functional high performing teams that are, by design, wired for education optimizations and 2. business models that reinforce equality of opportunity maximization.

By creating a remote, asynchronous product team that consists of product management, design and marketing power, software engineer power, and importantly instructional design and learning UX capacity, Sina is establishing a system for cross-functional problem and solution definition, to optimize pedagogy at a growing scale, sustainably and eventually profitably.

Learning Loop is currently made of 1 full-time member (Sina), 1 part-time Senior Product Designer, 1 part-time Mid-level Software Engineer, and 1 part-time Student Success manager.
